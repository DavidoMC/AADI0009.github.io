Privacy Policy

Stone Music respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information we collect about you. This privacy policy applies to our Discord music bot and explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information.

What information we collect:

  • We collect your Discord user ID in order to provide premium features.
  • We also collect your Discord guild ID in order to provide the bot's services to your Discord server.

How we use your information:

  • We use your Discord user ID to provide premium features, and your Discord guild ID to provide the bot's services to your Discord server.
  • We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except for payment processors (such as Patreon or PayPal) that are necessary to process premium subscriptions.

How we protect your information:

  • We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
  • All payment information is stored by Patreon or PayPal, and is subject to their privacy policies and security measures.

Changes to this policy:

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes to the policy, we will notify you by posting a notice on our website or through the bot itself.

Contact us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@stonemusic.in.
